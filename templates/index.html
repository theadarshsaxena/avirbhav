About JKIAPT

The J. K. Institute of Applied Physics and Technology is among the most prestigious education centres in the nation. The Department of Electronics and Communication, which is situated within the Muir College Campus (Science Faculty) of the University of Allahabad-is one of the four oldest universities in India. The foundation stone of the J.K. Institute was laid by Late Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru on January 14, 1949, and it was formally inaugurated by him on April 4 1956. J.K. charitable Trust's significant contribution to the building of the entire complex led to the identity it holds today.

In addition to providing courses in Electronics & Communication, the Department also teaches Computer Science. The courses have been tailored to meet the demands of the modern nation. The Department also offers B.Sc (Computer Science), M.Sc (Computer Science), M.Tech (Electronic Engineering), and M.Tech (Computer Technology) and D.Phil programs.

With a history spanning nearly 60 years, many alumni of the Department are making significant contributions in India and abroad. They are usually found to be at high positions in their respective fields all around the globe.

About Avirbhav

After spending two long years in the COVID lockdown, finally we are back in action with 'Avirbhav 2k22'. This year's theme breaks the traditional norm by centering around Harry Potter, mesmerizing all Potterheads. Events are well structured in a way to bring out the finest in all the students. The fest starts with a bang of online events and ends with a spectacular cultural clash between the four teams that are Ravenclaw, Gryffindor, Hufflepuff and Slytherin.

We are highly grateful to our seniors who are working tirelessly to make this event a memorable one.
